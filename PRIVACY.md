# Privacy Policy for FantasyEdge

**Last updated:** December 18, 2025

FantasyEdge ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data We Collect

### Yahoo Account Information

When you sign in with Yahoo, we receive:

- Your Yahoo user ID
- Your display name
- Your email address

This information is used solely to authenticate your session and identify your fantasy football leagues.

### Fantasy Football Data

We access the following data from your Yahoo Fantasy Football account:

- League information (league name, settings, scoring rules)
- Team information (team name, roster)
- Player data (players on your roster and available on waivers)

This data is used exclusively to generate personalized fantasy football recommendations.

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

- Authenticate your Yahoo Fantasy Football account
- Sync your league and roster information
- Generate start/bench recommendations for your lineup
- Identify waiver wire pickup opportunities
- Display recommendations directly on Yahoo Fantasy Football pages

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage:** Authentication tokens, user preferences, and cached recommendations are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
- **Server Processing:** League and roster data is transmitted to our secure Supabase servers to compute recommendations. This data is processed in real-time and not retained longer than necessary to provide the service.

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.**

Your data is only used to provide the extension's fantasy football recommendation features. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics beyond service improvement, or any purpose unrelated to the extension's core functionality.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- OAuth 2.0 is used for secure Yahoo authentication
- We do not store your Yahoo password

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained only while you actively use the extension
- You can delete all locally stored data by signing out within the extension
- Uninstalling the extension removes all local data
- Server-side data associated with your account can be deleted upon request

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the data we have about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out by uninstalling the extension

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
